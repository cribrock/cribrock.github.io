About Us

One trip to the super baby mega stores shows you that having a baby changes everything. When we had our first child, we found ourselves in a spot experienced by all new parents. How can you balance your old life of movies, hanging out with friends and dinners at nice restaurants with your new life of diapers, cooing at rattles and endless other delights?

And let's be honest. Who ever thought as parents we would be listening to hours of classical music just to entertain our baby? After our daughter was born, we searched around for music that was fun for her and didn't putus to sleep.

When we couldn't find exactly what we wanted, we realized here was a way to blend our old and new lives. We like to joke that just because music can stimulate your baby's development, there's no reason it should stunt yours!

We've developed a line of baby music where the songs have been picked for parents, but the music has been created for babies. In addition to our first album, Full Metal Diaper, which features instrumental versions of hard rock classics, CribRock albums will feature baby 80s pop, 90s grunge and pop, country, hip hop or other genres of music. Stay tuned!

Rob , Amy and Laine (our inspiration)
